Welcome to our newly redesigned home on the web.

E-Sparkman Magazine has been serving the St. Louis faith community since 1999. Our mission has never changed: to inspire, encourage, and inform the community we love. What has changed is our home — and we are proud to bring you a cleaner, faster, and more accessible experience.

Whether you are here for the Job Talk column, our Community Care resources, or the personal testimonies that have touched so many hearts, you will find it all here — organized, easy to read, and free, as always.

Thank you for being part of this community. We are just getting started.

— Marilyn Parker, Publisher & CEO
