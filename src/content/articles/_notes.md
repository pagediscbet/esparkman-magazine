# src/content/articles/

One markdown file per article. Filename becomes the URL slug.
Example: `workforce-trends-2026.md` → `/job-talk/workforce-trends-2026`

## Required frontmatter fields
```yaml
---
title: "Article Title Here"
author: "Author Full Name"
authorImage: "/images/articles/author-headshot.jpg"
category: "job-talk"           # must be one of the valid categories in config.ts
date: 2026-02-04
excerpt: "Short summary shown on article cards (1-2 sentences)."
image: "/images/articles/article-hero.jpg"
---
```

## Brand voice notes
- Warm, encouraging, community-focused tone
- Faith perspective is central — articles often reference God, scripture, or spiritual resilience
- Audience: primarily Black women aged 25–65 in the St. Louis metro area
- Write to uplift and inform, not to preach or lecture
- Keep language accessible — this is a community magazine, not academic writing
